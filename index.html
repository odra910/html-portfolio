The Best Movies According to Alexandra

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Trolls

Great songs, valid themes on the outlook on life being worse than reality;
Branch fearing danger, and Poppy having no sense of danger.

Princess Mononoki

Great story line about anger and the destruction of nature, beautiful
artwork that makes you long for a retreat into nature, but also in awe
of things greater than you, like the giant boar.

Sky High

Cute story line about having to live in your parents shadow of greatness
and making it your own way and discovering the greatness you have
in you thanks to your parents.
